Ingestion Guide

Document Ingestion translates raw specification manuals and images into high-dimensional vector embeddings, mapping them into specific Asset Namespaces in the Pinecone vector index.

Pydantic Constraints:
  • S3 Key: Letters, numbers, and / _ - . only.
  • Doc ID: Cannot be empty. Must match existing vectors for Update events.
  • Update Event: Must contain exactly one document for replacement.
Workflow:
  1. Select or enter your target Asset ID.
  2. Provide documents via direct upload, paste, or raw configuration.
  3. Submit to begin extraction, text parsing, embedding generation, and Pinecone loading.
